Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Contentflow ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Contentflow application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize our application through TikTok Login Kit, we may receive basic profile information including your TikTok Open ID, display name, and avatar URL. We do not collect any additional personal data beyond what TikTok provides during the OAuth authorization process.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we receive is used solely to authenticate your identity and enable the core functionality of our application, which is publishing video content to your own TikTok account. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

3. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data on external servers. Authentication tokens are stored locally and are only used to facilitate video publishing on your behalf. No user data is retained after your session ends.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy available at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy. We also use Pexels to source royalty-free video content, governed by Pexels' Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke our application's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account settings under "Manage App Permissions." Upon revocation, we will no longer be able to publish content on your behalf.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
